# UgorjBe Phase 4 art direction

## Direction: editorial mobility

Phase 4 combines the operational clarity expected from a mature mobility product with the warm, experience-led hierarchy of a local marketplace. It is not a car-sharing skin and it is not a clone of another offer application.

The result should feel:

- immediately understandable;
- premium without becoming cold;
- youthful without becoming childish;
- tactile without excessive decoration;
- editorial when browsing;
- calm and authoritative when booking.

## Brand relationship

The administration web established a credible UgorjBe language around deep forest, cream, paper, coral, sun and sage. Phase 4 makes the Android customer app part of the same product family while keeping mobile-specific density, edge-to-edge layout and touch feedback.

### Core palette roles

- **Forest:** navigation, operational map controls, trusted secondary actions.
- **Coral:** primary action, discount and selection emphasis.
- **Cream/Paper:** warm application and card surfaces.
- **Sun:** scarce positive accent for availability or warmth.
- **Sage:** calm supporting surfaces and selected navigation states.

Color is semantic. Error, success, selection and availability always include text, icon or shape in addition to color.

## Typography

Large discovery and campaign headings use Android's distributable serif fallback to introduce an editorial, human tone without committing proprietary font files. Body, metadata, prices and controls remain sans-serif for scanning speed.

- Serif: display and headline only.
- Sans: title, body, label and numeric operational information.
- Price, capacity and booking code use heavy sans hierarchy.
- Critical copy must survive 200% font scale.

## Geometry

Phase 3's very round visual language is reduced:

- 10 dp: compact supporting controls;
- 16 dp: input and primary control surfaces;
- 22 dp: cards and sheets;
- 28 dp: hero and navigation surfaces;
- pills only for true badges, filters and view switches.

The UgorjBe brand mark intentionally uses an asymmetric rounded shape to avoid a generic app-logo circle.

## Explore Map

- Full usable map canvas.
- Compact search/filter surface at the top.
- Current result count shown separately from controls.
- Price markers use UgorjBe forest; selected marker uses coral.
- Clusters use a clear count and strong white border.
- Marker selection opens an image-led controlled preview.
- Map/List switch remains obvious and reachable.
- Current markers stay visible during refresh.
- Camera motion has no continuous decorative animation.
- Missing Maps key provides a complete List route, not a dead end.

## Explore List

- Forest editorial header creates clear product identity.
- Cards prioritise image, time, provider, title, location, discount, price and capacity.
- Metadata is intentionally limited.
- Skeletons match final card geometry.
- Stable keys and preserved ViewModel state remain unchanged.
- Missing images produce category-specific artwork that still looks intentional.

## Authentication

- Forest brand canvas with warm abstract original shapes.
- One compact brand mark.
- Benefits communicate today, nearby and value without social-media mechanics.
- Login and registration use a segmented control rather than competing links.
- Form and debug credential hint remain separate visual layers.

## Detail and booking

The tested Phase 3 flow is preserved and recolored into the Phase 4 system:

- immersive experience image;
- strong editorial title;
- visible time and location;
- clear provider surface;
- compact support information;
- persistent price and booking action;
- server-authoritative review and capacity validation;
- finite success animation and meaningful haptic;
- visually dominant, accessible booking code.

## Secondary destinations

Bookings, favorites, providers and profile inherit the Phase 4 theme, typography, media artwork, navigation and semantic surfaces. Their information architecture remains stable to avoid unnecessary functional risk.

## Motion

- press scale: subtle and immediate;
- view change: 240 ms with eased fade/scale;
- detail navigation: shallow horizontal continuity rather than page-like sliding;
- sheet motion: platform Material behavior;
- success Lottie: finite;
- empty/loading Lottie: bounded to visible state;
- reduced motion: static final/native fallback;
- no background animation while the map camera moves.

## Originality checks

The final review must reject:

- a copied reference palette;
- vehicle-style markers or zone language;
- reference logos, screenshots or illustrations;
- distinctive card/navigation composition from another app;
- copied onboarding copy;
- a reference product's motion timing or signature interaction.

The art direction is approved only when the result is recognisable as UgorjBe without a reference product being named.
